--- book: Deadly Harvest page: 64 tags: wildfowl,all-other-fish,duck,turkey,chicken,chicken-breast-no-skin,goose ord: 1 --- ( . . . ) wildfowl and wild fish are just fine. Poultry, particularly chicken and turkey, tend to be fattier and contain more of the unhealthy fats. The breast (white meat) of the bird is the best, when it has the skin and fat removed, and free-range chickens tend to be leaner and healthier. Duck and goose are also fatty birds, but their fats are semi-liquid at room temperature, indicating a low saturated fat content.
