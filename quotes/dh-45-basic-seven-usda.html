--- book: Deadly Harvest page: 45 tags: usda,basic seven,green,yellow,orange,tomato,grapefruit,potato,fruit,milk,meat,poultry,fish,eggs,egg,peas,beans,bread,flour,cereals,butter,margarine --- It might be thought that this was very clear. However, in 1942, the USDA issued a new food guide that reduced the number of food groups to what they called the "Basic Seven." These were: green and yellow vegetables; oranges, tomatoes, and grapefruit; potatoes and other vegetables and fruit; milk and milk products; meat, poultry, fish, eggs, and dried peas and beans; bread, flour, and cereals; and butter and fortified margarine.
