--- book: Paleo in a Nutshell page: 77 tags: cocoa,chocolate-85-cocoa,flavanol,chocolate-dark ord: 2 --- in the vast majority of cocoa and chocolate products, the flavanols are destroyed during the roasting, fermenting, and processing of the cocoa beans. So, unless the product carries a credible flavonol claim, just eat very dark chocolate for the pleasure, not in the mistaken belief it will do you good, too!
