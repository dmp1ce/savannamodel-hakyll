--- book: Deadly Harvest page: 69 tags: peanut-oil,sunflower-oil,rapeseed-oil,canola-oil,safflower-oil,cottonseed-oil --- It is difficult to imagine, but just 100 years ago corn oil, peanut oil, sunflower oil, rapeseed oil (Canola oil), safflower oil, cottonseed oil, and other "vegetable" oils were virtually unknown to the ordinary consumer. They existed, of course, but only as an unwanted by-product of agricultural processes.
