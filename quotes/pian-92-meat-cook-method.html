--- book: Paleo in a Nutshell page: 92 tags: egg-non-omega3,egg,eggs,fish,poultry,olive-oil,all-other-fish ord: 1 --- Meat, poultry, eggs, and fish (Food Group 6) can be cooked using the most appropriate method: Microwaving, steaming, grilling, baking, or sauteing.
