--- book: Deadly Harvest page: 146 tags: watermelon, pineapple --- Many fruits, often of tropical origin, are high glycemic. They are not pariahs, but we should not go out of our way to obtain them. If you find a morsel or two in your fruit salad, swallow it down, it won't poison you. But do not consume these regularly or copiously. Examples are watermelon, pineapple, and ripe banana.
