--- book: Paleo in a Nutshell page: 70 tags: chicken-breast-no-skin,chicken,turkey,duck,goose,pheasant,grouse,pigeon,turkey-breast-no-skin --- The low-fat parts of farm fowl, such as skinless chicken and turkey breast, are good in modest quantities. You should reduce consumption of other parts as much as possible. All parts of duck and goose are fine. Wild birds such as pheasant, grouse, and pigeon are fully conforming.
