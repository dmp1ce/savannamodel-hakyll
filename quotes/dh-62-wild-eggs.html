--- book: Deadly Harvest page: 62 tags: egg,eggs,egg-omega3,wild eggs,wild,quail,pigeon,gull,lapwing,plover,pheasant,ostrich,egg-quail --- The gathering of wild eggs today is greatly restricted by government regulation in most developed countries. However, the eggs of many species are available in small quantities as a by-product of the management of game birds. In this way, eggs from quail, pigeons, gulls, lapwings, plovers, pheasants, and ostriches are available to culinary enthusiasts.
