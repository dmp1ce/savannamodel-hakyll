--- book: Paleo in a Nutshell page: 83 tags: omega-3,canola-oil,olive-oil,rapeseed-oil,hempseed-oil,flax-oil,colza,hemp-oil --- Be frugal with fats and oils, even the "Green" classes. Never cook at high temperatures. Only bake or sauté at low temperatures— less than 320°F (160°C), although this may be a little higher for baking. Only use olive oil or canola (rapeseed) oil. Hot or cold, replace "Red" classes of fats and oils with "Green" classes such as olive oil, canola (rapeseed, colza) oil, hempseed oil, and flax oils ...
