--- book: Deadly Harvest page: 130 tags: farmed-fish, salmon, trout, omega-3, omega3 --- all is not well with farmed fish, such as salmon and trout: their omega-3 content depends on what they are fed. Even though they are carnivores, farmed salmon are fed with pellets made of grains, fish meal, and vegetable oil, and so their flesh is often deficient in the omega-3 oils.
