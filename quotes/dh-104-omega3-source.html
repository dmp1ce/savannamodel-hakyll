--- book: Deadly Harvest page: 104 tags: omega3,omega-3,walnut,flaxseed,hempseed,rapeseed,canola-oil,sardine,salmon,trout,tuna,fresh-bluefin-tuna,tuna-canned,tuna-except-bluefin --- Omega-3 EFAs are found in plants and animal matter. In plants, the most common form is alpha-linolenic acid (ALA), found particularly in walnuts, flaxseed, hempseed, and rapeseed (canola oil). In animals, omega-3 oils are particularly found in "oily" fish, such as sardines, salmon, trout, and tuna.
