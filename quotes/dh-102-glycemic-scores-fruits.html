--- book: Deadly Harvest page: 102 tags: pineapple, banana, raspberry --- Glycemic index scores present a few surprises. Starchy foods like bread (even whole-wheat) and breakfast cereal (corn flakes) are "bad" carbohydrates. Fruits are all over the place: pineapple is "bad," banana is "borderline," and raspberries are "favorable." Unsurprisingly, non-starchy, non-sugary foods like most nuts, salads, and vegetables fit into the "favorable" category
