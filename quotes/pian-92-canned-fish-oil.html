--- book: Paleo in a Nutshell page: 92 tags: oily-fish,sardine,olive-oil,water,salmon,kipper,canola-oil,salt ord: 2 --- With regard to animal matter, many methods of conservation are acceptable. Canned oily fish (such as sardines) are, in nutritional terms, just as good as fresh. Just choose the versions that are preserved in olive oil, canola oil, or unsalted water. Smoked salmon or kipper are good, although watch out for high salt content.
