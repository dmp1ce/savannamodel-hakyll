--- book: Deadly Harvest page: 46 tags: milk,meat,fish,poultry,egg,egg-non-omega3,bean,nut,fruit,grain,orange,tomato,grapefruit --- In 1956, the "Basic Seven" groups were condensed to the "Basic Four": milk and milk products; meat, fish, poultry, eggs, dry beans, and nuts; fruits and vegetables; and grains. This time the "green and yellow vegetables" group has disappeared. "Butter and fortified margarine" has been dropped. "Oranges, tomatoes, and grapefruit" are lumped into the catch-all category "fruits and vegetables."
