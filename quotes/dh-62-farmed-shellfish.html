--- book: Deadly Harvest page: 62 tags: shellfish, mussel, oyster, shrimp, prawn, clam, crayfish,all-other-shellfish,mussels,prawns ord: 3 --- The farming of shellfish, mainly mussels, oysters, shrimps, and prawns, has been carried out on a minor scale for centuries in Europe and Japan. Again, since the 1970s, rapid advances in technology have allowed the farm production of shrimp and prawns to explode. They have elbowed out the wild variety. The farming of clams, crayfish, oysters, and mussels is also growing fast.
