--- book: Deadly Harvest page: 164 tags: salad,salad-dressing,vinaigrette,eggs,egg,tomato,mushroom,sausage,steak,hash-brown,toast,waffle,syrup,muffin --- Eating in fast-food restaurants is just the same, only harder. Resign yourself to ordering the burger and throwing away the bun. Find a salad, if you can, but discard the packet of salad dressing (consider carrying a bottle of your own vinaigrette). Or eat the all-day breakfast: eggs with tomatoes and mushrooms are acceptable, but avoid the sausage, steak, hash-browns, toast, waffles, syrup, and muffins.
