--- book: Deadly Harvest page: 65 tags: almond, walnut, pistachio, chestnut,fertile-crescent,brazil-nut,cashew,pecan,macadamia --- Almonds, walnuts, pistachios, and chestnuts are all native to the Fertile Crescent and were domesticated early during the farming revolution. The Brazil nut and the cashew nut are native to South America, the pecan to North America, and the macadamia to Queensland in Australia, and all of these nuts have become familiar to us in the West. They are often processed in various ways, notably by roasting and salting, which improves shelf life and taste, but it is not a nutritional improvement.
