--- book: Deadly Harvest page: 15 tags: women,locust,caterpillar,grub,toad,tortoise,snake,egg-omega3,egg,ostrich eggs --- The women also collect eggs of all kinds and capture small animals such as locusts, caterpillars, grubs, toads, tortoises, and snakes. Ostrich eggs are particularly valued. The contents supply a good portion of food, the shells make containers for water, and bits of shell are carved into beads.
